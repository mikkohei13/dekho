Reading the heatmap

Co-occurrence (like-weighted) — Cell brightness shows how often two tags appear on the same track, weighted by user like rating (like3 = 4×, like2 = 3×, like1 = 2×, like0 = 1×, not-like = 0×, unrated = 1×). Diagonal shows each tag's total weighted frequency.

Co-occurrence (raw count) — Simple count of tracks where both tags appear together.

Surprise (log₂ observed/expected) — Positive values (warm colors) mean the pair appears together more often than expected by chance — an unexpected or distinctive combination. Negative values (cool colors) mean the pair appears less often than expected — tags that tend to avoid each other.
